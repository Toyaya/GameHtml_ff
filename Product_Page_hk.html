Game Time Cards

The 60 Day Game Time Cards are available at participating video game retailers throughout North America and serve as a payment option allowing players to add 60 days of game time at the Standard subscription rate, subject to account limits.

Sixty days of game time will be added to your account once you redeem the 20-digit PIN code found on the card itself or on the purchase receipt through Mog Station, the FINAL FANTASY XIV Account Management System.

* Game Time Cards must be redeemed on Mog Station in order to receive the 60 days of game time.
